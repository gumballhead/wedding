Getting there

The wedding will be held at Hilton Playa del Carmen, an all-inclusive, adults-only resort. If you are travelling with minors, there are other family-friendly resorts close by. I would look close to the 5th Avenue area on the beach.

Guests who are staying at another resort will have access to the Hilton during the wedding ceremony. The Hilton does not provide or sell wristbands to non-guests outside of the ceremony, but Playa del Carmen is a very safe area and there are plenty of things around to do so we won't be isolated at separate resorts the whole week.

You can book a package deal that includes flights on expedia.com. This is normally the cheapest way to book your trip. Flight prices are highly variable, so we use Google Flights and set up a flight tracker to get notifications when prices drop.

If you're coming from Des Moines, you can usually save yourself a good $500 or so by leaving from Minneapolis or Kansas City. Minneapolis will even go direct to Cancun. There are stay and fly hotels in Minneapolis that will let you park for free the whole week and also provide a free shuttle to the airport.

Once you arrive in Cancun, you'll need to get transportation to Playa del Carmen, which is about 45 minutes away. If you booked through Expedia, they should contact you to arrange a shuttle in the weeks before your trip. They'll be walking around wearing Expedia jackets outside the airport, so they're easy to find. Otherwise, there are taxis available outside the airport. You'll probably also want to book your return flight in the afternoon to give yourself plenty of time to get back to the airport.
